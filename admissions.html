Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Visit

Submit an inquiry form and schedule a campus tour to experience our learning environment firsthand.

  • Online inquiry form submission
  • Campus tour scheduling
  • Virtual tour option available
2

Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documents.

  • Online application form
  • Previous school records
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Passport-sized photographs
3

Assessment & Interview

Participate in age-appropriate assessments and family interviews.

  • Academic assessment (Grades 2+)
  • Student interview
  • Parent meeting
  • Observation for younger students
4

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment formalities.

  • Decision notification within 2 weeks
  • Fee payment and documentation
  • Orientation program
  • Uniform and book purchase

Admission Requirements

Application Form

Completed online application form with all required information.

Academic Records

Previous two years' report cards and transfer certificate (if applicable).

Identification

Birth certificate copy and passport-sized photographs of student.

Health Records

Immunization records and recent medical examination report.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit Scholarships

Academic excellence scholarships for students with outstanding academic records.

  • Up to 50% tuition waiver
  • Annual renewal based on performance
  • Available for Grades 6-12

Talent Scholarships

Scholarships for students demonstrating exceptional talent in sports, arts, or music.

  • Up to 30% tuition waiver
  • Portfolio/audition required
  • All grade levels eligible

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for families demonstrating financial need.

  • Sliding scale based on income
  • Confidential application process
  • Documentation required

Sibling Discount

Discount for families with multiple children enrolled.

  • 10% discount for second child
  • 15% discount for third child
  • 20% discount for fourth+ child
